About Me
I am a health journalist and data visualization designer, passionate about equitable health policy and systems.
After earning my MPH from Columbia University, I led advocacy strategy for global health organizations and campaigns for affordable health care in New York. Now, I tell data-driven public health stories using Python, Flourish, RAWGraphs, QGIS, Datawrapper, Adobe Illustrator, D3.js + more with training from the Lede Program.
